DJ Alok

DJ Alok's character has an exceptional ability in the game called 'Drop The Beat.' This ability creates a 5m aura that increases the ally movement speed by 10% and restores 5 HP/s for 5 seconds. It is an active ability that is best-suited to aggressive players and can be boosted up to level 6 with character level-up cards. The max level provides the ability to increase the ally movement speed by 15% and to restore HP by 5HP/s for 10 seconds.

DJ Alok can be bought from Free Fire's in-game store for 599 diamonds.
